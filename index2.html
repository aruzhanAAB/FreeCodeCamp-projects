Meringue roll

Meringue roll is a fairly simple, but very impressive cake that will leave no one indifferent.

Ingredients

  • Egg white 200 g
  • Citric acid 2 g
  • Powdered sugar 330 g
  • Starch 20 g
  • Almond petals 25 g
  • Cottage cheese 150 g
  • Cream 33% ml
  • Pistachio paste 25g
  • Raspberry puree 100g
  • Fresh raspberries 30 g
  • Fresh mint 1 sprig

Instructions

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Measure out exactly 200g. Add 300g of powdered sugar and citric acid. Whip the meringue: first at low speed until light foam forms, then at high speed until stiff peaks form. Using a spatula, fold 40g of starch into the finished meringue.
  2. Line a baking sheet with a silicone mat. Spread a thin, even layer of the meringue on the mat. Sprinkle with flaked almonds and bake the meringue in a preheated oven at 160 degrees Celsius (325 degrees Fahrenheit) for 35 minutes.
  3. In a saucepan, combine the raspberry puree and cornstarch and mix thoroughly. Heat the mixture over medium heat until it comes to a gentle simmer, stirring constantly. Remove the raspberry filling from the heat and pour it into a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until completely cool, then transfer to a pastry bag.
  4. For the filling, combine the cream cheese, powdered sugar, pistachio paste, and heavy cream in a bowl. Beat with a mixer until smooth and thick. Transfer the cream to a pastry bag fitted with a closed star tip and refrigerate.
  5. Remove the finished meringue from the oven and let it cool. Cover it with a second baking sheet lined with a silicone mat. Turn the baking sheet over so the silicone mat is facing up. Carefully remove the mat from the meringue.
  6. Spread the pistachio cream (reserve some for decoration) and raspberry filling over the meringue. Roll it into a roulade.
  7. Trim the edges of the meringue and transfer the roll to a serving plate. Garnish with the remaining pistachio cream, fresh raspberries, and mint. Serve.
